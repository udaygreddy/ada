# ADA (ADP Discovery Agent)
## Comprehensive Client Discovery & Document Collection Checklist

---

# 1. Sales & Contracting

## Documents
- Sales Order (signed)
- Master Service Agreement (MSA)
- Statement of Work (SOW)
- Any addendums / amendments

---

# 2. Financial & Client Verification

## Documents
- Federal Employer Identification Number (EIN) proof

### Bank Account Verification
- Bank letter
- Voided check

### Financial Verification
- D&B / credit check documentation
- Direct debit authorization

---

# 3. Payroll & Core Data

## A. Company & Workforce Data

### Documents / Data
- Company-level data (EDGE / discovery output)
- Employee Masterfile (Excel/CSV)
- Demographics
- Job/compensation data
- Organizational structure

---

## B. Payroll Configuration Data

### Documents / Data
- Earnings codes & setup
- Deduction codes & setup
- Tax setup / configuration (federal, state, local)

---

## C. Historical Payroll Data

### Documents / Data
- Year-to-date (YTD) balances
- Payroll registers (prior periods)
- Payroll summary reports
- Sample payroll outputs from prior vendor

---

## D. Employee-level Payroll Artifacts

### Documents
- Paystubs
- W-2s

---

# 4. Tax & Compliance

## A. Tax Setup & Historical Data

### Documents
- Prior quarter tax returns (e.g., 941, 940, SUI, SIT)
- Proof of tax deposits
- Existing tax filings

---

## B. Authorization & Compliance Forms

### Documents
- Power of Attorney (POA) forms (state/federal)
- Client Authorization Agreement (CAA)
- Reporting Agent Authorization (RAA)
- Tax & banking recap approval

---

# 5. General Ledger (GL)

## A. Core Accounting Inputs

### Documents / Data
- Chart of accounts
- Recent journal entries
- Payroll journal samples

---

## B. System Integration Details

### Documents / Data
- Accounting software name
- GL import file specifications / format
- File layout requirements
- Accounting calendar

---

# 6. Time & Attendance

## A. Policy Configuration

### Documents / Data
- Time & attendance policies:
  - Overtime rules
  - Rounding rules
  - Work periods
- Time-off policies (PTO, sick, vacation)

---

## B. Employee Setup

### Documents / Data
- Employee-to-policy mappings
- Time approvers / manager hierarchy
- Time zones and locations

---

## C. Supporting Data

### Documents / Data
- Timesheets (historical or sample)
- Time clock/device setup details

---

# 7. Benefits & Carrier Connections

## A. Plan & Eligibility Setup

### Documents / Data
- Benefit eligibility class definitions
- Plan design documentation
- Medical, dental, vision
- HSA, FSA, wellness programs

---

## B. Enrollment & Population Data

### Documents / Data
- Employee-to-plan mappings
- Dependent information
- Enrollment data (active elections)

---

## C. Payroll & Validation Inputs

### Documents / Data
- Current deductions (from payroll)
- Benefit deduction comparisons

---

## D. Carrier Integration

### Documents / Data
- Carrier/vendor details
- Connection/file requirements

---

## E. Supporting Documents

### Documents
- Benefits plan documents (PDFs)
- Benefits FAQ / employee-facing materials

---

# 8. Talent (Recruiting, Onboarding, Performance, Compensation)

## A. Recruiting

### Documents
- Job descriptions
- Requisition templates
- Job board integrations / links

---

## B. Onboarding

### Documents
- New hire packet

- Onboarding forms

## C. Employee Documentation

### Documents

## D. Performance Management

- Performance review templates

## E. Compensation

### Documents / Data
- Compensation plans

### Documents / Data

## C. Document Management

### Documents

# 12. Third-Party / Supporting Documentation

### Documents
